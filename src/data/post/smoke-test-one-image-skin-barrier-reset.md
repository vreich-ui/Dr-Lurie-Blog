### A calmer routine starts with fewer products

When skin feels tight or reactive, the most useful first move is often a short reset. Keep the routine simple enough that the skin has a chance to settle and you can see what is actually helping.

### The three-day baseline

For a few days, use a gentle cleanser, a plain moisturizer, and morning sunscreen. Pause exfoliating acids, scrubs, and fragrance-heavy extras while you watch for changes in comfort, redness, and tightness.

### What the reset tells you

If the skin feels calmer, add one active back at a time. If irritation returns quickly, the pattern is useful information rather than a reason to push harder.

### Plan the next step

A short consultation can help turn the pattern into a simpler product plan, especially when the routine has become hard to read.
