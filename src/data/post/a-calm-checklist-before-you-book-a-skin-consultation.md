### A Calm Checklist Before You Book a Skin Consultation

When a skin concern starts to feel bigger than your bathroom mirror can answer, the next helpful step is often a focused consultation. This short guide explains what to notice before you book, what to bring, and how to make the visit feel less rushed.

### Start With What Has Actually Changed

A useful consultation begins with specifics: when the concern started, whether it comes and goes, what you have already tried, and what seems to make it better or worse. You do not need a perfect timeline. A few honest notes are often enough to make the conversation clearer.

### Bring The Products You Really Use

Patients sometimes feel they should edit their routine before a visit. It is more helpful to bring the real list: cleanser, moisturizer, sunscreen, prescriptions, supplements, and any active ingredients used in the past month. That gives Dr. Lurie a grounded view of what your skin is already experiencing.
