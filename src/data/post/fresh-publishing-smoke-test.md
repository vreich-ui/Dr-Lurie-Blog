# Fresh Publishing Smoke Test: A Clean Path to Live

This article is a fresh alpha test of the publishing pipeline. It exists to confirm that a new article can move from generated content to a completed workflow record and then into the publishing queue without relying on old state.

The text is intentionally simple. It makes no medical, legal, financial, or current-event claims. That keeps the test focused on the mechanics that matter here: article metadata, final-stage completion, image handling, and immediate publication.

The image assets for this run are treated as first-class publishing records. The full-size hero image and the thumbnail are uploaded separately, labeled clearly, and attached to the final article output with their server-returned artifact references.

A successful run should leave a clean trail: a request ID, a completed final article stage, verified image uploads, and a publish result that names the article path and deployment state.
