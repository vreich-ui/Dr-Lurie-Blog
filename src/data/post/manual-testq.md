The transition into perimenopause often brings unexpected changes to the skin. One of the most common experiences shared is a sudden increase in skin reactivity—feelings of irritation, redness, and dryness that weren’t present before. This shift can feel perplexing and concerning, but it is rooted in natural biological processes.
