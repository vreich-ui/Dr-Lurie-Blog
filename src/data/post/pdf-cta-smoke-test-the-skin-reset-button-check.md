### When a skin routine needs a reset

A reset does not have to mean throwing away every product or starting from scratch. Sometimes the most useful move is to make the next seven days simpler, easier to observe, and less emotionally loaded.

### What to watch for

During a short reset, the useful patterns are often ordinary: when tightness appears, whether redness clusters after a certain step, and whether the skin feels calmer when fewer variables are changing at once.

### Why the download matters in this test

This article includes a PDF guide because the smoke test needs a visible button that uses the generated download destination. The default article CTA should stay out of the way when the PDF CTA is present.
