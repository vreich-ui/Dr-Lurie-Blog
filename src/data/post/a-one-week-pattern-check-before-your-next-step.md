### A Clearer Week Beats a Bigger Guess

When symptoms feel inconsistent, it is tempting to jump straight to a sweeping plan. A cleaner first move is to notice what repeats across an ordinary week. This smoke-test article uses a short PDF worksheet as the main next step so the published page can verify a real download CTA.

### What Seven Days Can Show

Seven days is short enough to finish and long enough to reveal small patterns: time of day, triggers, recovery moments, and questions worth bringing into care. The goal is not to diagnose yourself from a worksheet. It is to make the next conversation more specific.

### What To Track

- When the symptom appeared
- What was happening just before it changed
- How intense it felt in plain language
- What helped, even a little
- What you want to ask next
